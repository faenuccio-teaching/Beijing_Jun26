      Heights of rational numbers #

      Since ℚ has a unique infinite place (the usual absolute value) and every finite place satisfies v n ≤ 1 for n : ℕ, the height simplifies to mulHeight₁ (n : ℚ) = n and logHeight₁ (n : ℚ) = Real.log n for 1 ≤ n.

      source

      Height.mulHeight₁ ↑n = ↑n

      The multiplicative height of a positive natural number cast to ℚ equals n.
